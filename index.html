Introducing SEMILLAS

Empowering Future Generations

SEMILLAS is dedicated to providing K-8 students with the tools and skills they need for a successful future. Our mission is to make a positive impact on our communities by equipping students with the knowledge and abilities to tackle environmental challenges head-on.

Through innovative teaching practices, family engagement, and community partnerships, we ensure that every child has the tools to thrive in a changing world. SEMILLAS connects science learning with real-world environmental challenges, fostering climate literacy and informed decision-making among multilingual learners.

Our Partners

Collaborations with Top Universities

SEMILLAS thrives through strong partnerships with leading research institutions. Through collaborations with the University of Missouri and Mississippi State University, we deliver high-quality, culturally sustaining science education to local schools.

These partnerships enhance teacher learning, engage families, and connect classrooms to real-world challenges. Working with experts in climate science, linguistics, and STEM pedagogy, we co-design innovative, research-based practices that empower multilingual learners and ensure science education is equitable, relevant, and transformative.
